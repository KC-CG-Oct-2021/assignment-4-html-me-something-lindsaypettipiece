We come from a long line of strong, beautiful women. This is a brief story of our matriarchs.

Alice Webster is your Great Great Grandmother, and she was married to Roy Webster. They had two daughters, your Great Grandma Mary and your Great Great Aunt Dolores.

Mary Webster met a handsome young man named Herman Metcalf,

and they just couldn't wait to be married so they eloped to Reno, Nevada and tied the knot!

They wasted no time starting a family...

Their first born was your Nana Kathleen

Your Nana Kathleen grew up in sunny California with her brother and sister. Your Great Uncle Richard and your Gret Aunt Bonnie

And then one day your Nana Kathleen and your Grandpa Guy met and decide they wanted to get married! So they also decided to elope to Reno, Nevada!

This is where I come into the picture! I was born February 8th, 1985 in Santa Clara, California. I had one older brother, your Uncle Bryan. We grew up in Fremont, Californian and had many great adventures as a family.

Many years later I decided to go on a new adventure all by myself to Kansas City, Missouri! This adventure would give me so much more then I could ever ask for. This adventure is the adventure that gave me you!
